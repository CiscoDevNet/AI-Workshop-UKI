ARK Industries — IT Policy Summary
Version: 3.2 | Last updated: March 2026
Approved by: IT Director, Diane Okafor

AI TOOL USAGE POLICY
─────────────────────
Approved AI tools (as of March 2026):
  - Microsoft Copilot (enterprise licence — all staff)
  - Claude Teams (IT and engineering departments only)

Prohibited actions with any AI tool:
  - Uploading customer data, intellectual property, or regulated data to any AI tool
  - Using personal/consumer AI accounts on work devices for work tasks
  - Inputting financial forecasts, M&A information, or board-level documents

Requirements:
  - All AI-generated content must be reviewed by a human before use in official documents
  - Any new AI tool must be approved by the IT Director before use
  - AI tool usage is logged via Cisco AI Access for compliance audit purposes

PASSWORD AND ACCESS POLICY
───────────────────────────
  - Minimum password length: 16 characters
  - MFA required: all remote access and all cloud services (no exceptions)
  - Privileged access review: quarterly — managed by Marcus Webb
  - Contractor access: granted for project duration only, reviewed monthly

DEVICE POLICY
──────────────
  - All managed devices must be enrolled in Cisco Meraki MDM within 24 hours of provisioning
  - BYOD: personal devices permitted for Webex and email via managed app only
  - USB storage: prohibited on all managed devices since January 2026
  - Lost or stolen devices: report immediately to security@ark-industries.co.uk and IT Director

INCIDENT REPORTING
───────────────────
  - All security incidents: report to security@ark-industries.co.uk within 1 hour of discovery
  - P1 incidents: escalate immediately to IT Director (ext. 4400) and CISO
  - Post-incident review required within 5 business days for all P1 and P2 incidents
  - Near-misses: encouraged to report via the same channel — no blame culture
