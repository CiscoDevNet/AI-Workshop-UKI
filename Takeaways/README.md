# Takeaways — Cisco AI Technical Workshop

## This folder is your Monday morning kit. The workshop is over, but the work starts now.

Everything you built today — the AI assistant, the agentic workflow, the draft governance policy — lives on your laptop and in this folder. The session is finished; the practical journey is just beginning. This folder contains three files designed to help you take the first steps back in your own organisation, without needing to remember everything from today and without needing anyone's permission to start.

---

## What Is in This Folder

| File | What It Is | When to Use It |
|---|---|---|
| `monday_morning_action_plan.md` | Five concrete, low-cost experiments you can run in the next two weeks using only the tools from today — no new software, no budget required. | Open this on Monday morning when you are wondering where to start. |
| `manager_brief_email_template.md` | A ready-to-personalise email you can send to your line manager or leadership team to propose a 30-day internal pilot programme based on what you learned today. | Use this within the next week, while the day is still fresh and the conversation is easy to start. |
| `cisco_learning_resources.md` | A curated, link-bearing list of next-step learning resources — from Cisco U and DevNet to Jan AI documentation and Tavily — so you know exactly where to go when you want to go deeper. | Keep this as a reference and share it with colleagues who were not in the room today. |
