# Session 1 — Group Activity Worksheet
## The Prompt Challenge: Designing an AI Assistant

---

### How This Works

You are working in groups of **3–4 people**. You have **15 minutes**.

No laptops needed. No AI tools. This is a thinking exercise — and that is deliberate.

For each scenario below, your group will answer three questions together. There are no wrong answers. Think out loud. Draw on what you know from your own experience.

**Your scenario is printed on the card at your table.** Use this worksheet to capture your group's thinking.

---

> **One rule:** to do this well, you have to apply real IT knowledge. Generic answers will not get you far. The scenario is specific — so should be your answers.

---

## Scenario A — Firmware Compliance Audit

### The Situation

You are a Senior Network Engineer at ARK Industries. A critical firmware vulnerability has just been published affecting the Cisco Meraki MX security appliances — the same model ARK Industries runs across 38 of its 52 locations. Your team lead wants a written brief by end of day covering the risk, which sites are affected, and the recommended remediation steps — written clearly enough to be forwarded to the IT Director without further explanation.

You want to use an AI assistant to help you research and draft that brief.
